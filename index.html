Understanding is the first step towards tolerance and social diversity: cannabis is native to our planet; at worst it is a benign plant and at its potential it is a medicinal herb with known spiritual effects. The issues associated with cannabis have stemmed through the criminalization of it and its users. These actions are destroying our communities. It is our vision to counteract these destructive actions by working within our communities to promote social action and community service.
If you have a doctor's recommendation to use cannabis as a medicine, pursuant to the provisions of California Health and Safety Code § 11362.775, we would like to invite you to join our Patient Member Co-op.
